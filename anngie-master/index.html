About me

Anngie Dehoyos is a Bay Area transplant from Los Angeles whose devoted most of her career to championing music and telling the stories of those who make it. Throughout the last 10 years, she's contributed to a variety of publications, including MP3.com, CBS Radio's, Live 105 and KROQ, TV.com and Last.fm.
